OUR PROJECTS

NEW YORK CITY

The picture shown here is a rendering of our first commercial facility,to be built on Building 3 of the Brooklyn Navy Yard. The system is modelled after EBF Gmbhs Aquaponic Solar Greenhouse and offers the following benefits over traditional agriculture:

  • 64% Increased Crop Production
  • No Chemical Pesticides or Fertilizers
  • 90% Less Water Usage and Zero Run-off
  • Rainwater Harvesting Prevents Sewerage Overflow
  • Net-zero Annual Energy Consumption
  • The goals of this project include:
  • Jumpstarting our Integrated CSA
  • Establishing our First Hands-on Educational Facility
  • Creating a Presence at the Brooklyn Navy Yard
  • Generating Momentum through High Visibility
  • Gathering Metrics to Prove Environmental Benefits
The produce and fish that we grow in this facility will be distributed directly to bars and restaurants within the greater NYC metropolitan area.
